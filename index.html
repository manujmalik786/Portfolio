About Me

Designer, strategist, and lifelong problem solver.

I'm obsessed with understanding how people actually use products. Not how we think they should use them—but how they really do. This curiosity drives everything I do.

Over the past decade, I've learned that the best design comes from deep empathy, clear strategy, and measurable outcomes. I don't believe in design for design's sake. Every pixel should serve a purpose.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring how complex systems work, reading about behavioral psychology, or experimenting with new tools. I'm endlessly curious and genuinely excited about helping teams build products that matter.

How I Work

Research-first, strategy-driven, always measuring impact. I push back on assumptions and celebrate data-backed decisions.

What I Believe

Great design solves real problems. Complexity is the enemy. Consistency scales teams. Collaboration beats solo brilliance.

Why I Care

Products shape how billions of people live and work. That responsibility excites me. I want to build things that genuinely improve lives.
