About

I simplify complexity without flattening nuance.

I am a UI/UX designer who enjoys ambiguous problems. My approach combines structured research, collaborative decision-making, and visual precision to build products people understand quickly.

I care about what users do, not only what they say. I test assumptions early, document decisions clearly, and design for edge cases from day one.
