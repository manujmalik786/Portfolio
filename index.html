About Me

I started as a curious designer. Now I solve problems strategically.

My journey into design began with a simple question: Why do people struggle with products that are supposed to help them? This led me deeper into research, psychology, and systems thinking—and eventually to a Master's degree in Design.

Throughout my career, I've noticed a pattern: the best products aren't designed in isolation. They're built through collaboration, grounded in research, and refined through multiple iterations. I've learned that a single design decision can move business metrics, transform team culture, or even save users time and frustration.

Today, I partner with leaders who care about both craft and outcomes. I bring research rigor, design strategy, and execution excellence to solve the hardest product problems.

What Drives Me

  • Research over opinions. Data informs decisions.
  • Systems over heroics. Good design scales.
  • Clarity over complexity. Simplicity is hard work.
  • Outcomes over aesthetics. Beauty that works.
  • Teams over individuals. Collaboration multiplies impact.
